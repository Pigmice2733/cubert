driver:
    left y-axis - drive up/down
    right x-axis - turn left/right

operator:
    left trigger - manually raise intake
    right trigger - manually lower intake
    d-pad up - move intake to up position
    d-pad left or right - move intake to mid position
    d-pad down - move intake to low position
    a - spin up shooter to low speed
    y - spin up shooter to high speed
    x - feed shooter
    **b - toggle intake wheels** (might have the intake wheels automatically toggle)

    * = not implemented yet
    ** = might not use
